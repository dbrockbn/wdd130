Earthquakes and Disasters

We rely on clean water more than most of us would like to realize. The average american household uses about 80 gallons per person per day. We need to clean our bodies, our food, our dishes, clothes, and homes. More importantly we need water to drink and cook with. A recommended standard is to store a gallon per person per day for emergencies. For a family of 5 for one week that would be 35 gallons of water, which would only cover drinking water.
